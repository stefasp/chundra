Totem of Legacy – cycle of life

This piece speaks of the cycle of life. The figures could represent one person—youth and age—or two, reflecting how life is passed from one to another. It’s a reminder that we don’t completely leave when we go; we leave parts of ourselves in those we love, in those who love us, and in the things we build and leave behind. Like a totem, it stands tall, carrying the weight of memories, of connections, of legacy. Its light shines not just in the present but echoes through the lives touched and the moments shared.
