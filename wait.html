Wait for me

What I love about these materials is how the limits of the shape feel broken. You can sense some structure, but at the same time it’s blurred — everything seems a bit mixed, as if it all belongs to the same thing.

I guess that’s how life feels to me. We are one, but everything that touches us can get inside of us — and at the same time, we send our own vibes and energy into our surroundings.

I wish to be able to charge myself with the kind of energy I need to stay calm and joyful — to send “happy” vibes no matter what I’m surrounded by. We often try to surround ourselves only with what feels good, but life is more than that — and a true gift is to stay true to ourself no matter what.
