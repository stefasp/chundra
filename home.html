About Me

I was born in Uruguay and raised in San Telmo, Argentina—a bohemian neighborhood known for its colonial and gothic architecture, cobblestone streets, and vibrant art scene. Growing up surrounded by antiques and street art, I developed a deep appreciation for art, philosophy, and creativity. Always a nocturnal soul, I spent my nights immersed in drawing, reading, and painting, developing a spiritual connection through meditation that infuses my work with a sense of magic and wonder.


As a self-taught artist, my passion and dedication have fueled my growth. My art reflects my personal journey, blending vibrant colors and surreal textures that evoke the mysteries of both the inner and outer worlds and the ways they intersect. Now living in Málaga, Spain, I continue to evolve my craft, drawing inspiration from the Mediterranean lifestyle and my travels.
