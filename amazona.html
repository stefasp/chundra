Amazona

She embodies the feminine with grace, with power, carrying an ancient energy that feels almost otherworldly. Amazona is a creature from another planet, for the feminine has been so long disqualified in this one that when it rises within us, it feels like a hidden, distant, precious force. Surrounded by petals, she watches us, studies us, and invites us to question the feminine—what it means, what it hides, and what it holds. She is both the question and the answer, a cosmic reminder of the strength and beauty that lie within the feminine mystery.
